I've wanted a place of my own to think out loud for a while, so here it is.

This site is where I'll write about the subjects I keep circling back to:
politics, science fiction, economics, and the ideas that sit where they
overlap. Some posts will be short notes; others will be longer essays. The
common thread is that they're all things I find genuinely interesting and want
to talk through.

If something here sparks a thought — agreement, disagreement, or a tangent —
I'd love to hear it. You can find my email on the [about](/about) page.
